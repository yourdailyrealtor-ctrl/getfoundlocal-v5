FAQ

Common questions

Do I really own the website?+

Yes — fully. Domain in your name, all files are yours. The monthly plan covers hosting and maintenance, not ownership.

Do I pay before seeing the site?+

No. We build it and send you a preview first. Zero payment until you've seen it and approved it.

What if I want to cancel?+

Cancel anytime. Your domain will eventually expire but we'll send you all files so you keep the site.

How long does it take?+

Usually 3–4 days from approval to a live site. Most agencies take 4–6 weeks.
